Privacy Policy

Effective: March 24, 2026
🔐

Our Commitment

Gold Hunter AR ("the Game") is developed by an independent developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the Game accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Key Principle: All game data is processed and stored locally on your device. We do not operate any external servers and do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.
📍

Location Data (GPS)

The Game requires access to your device's GPS/location services to provide the core augmented reality gameplay experience — detecting virtual metals based on your real-world position.

  • Location data is processed entirely on your device
  • No coordinates are sent to any external server
  • Location access is used only during active gameplay
  • You can revoke location permission at any time via your device settings
📷

Camera Access (AR)

The Game uses your device's camera to render augmented reality content (dig spots, visual effects, and 3D overlays) on real-world surfaces using ARCore (Android) technology.

  • Camera feed is processed on-device only by the AR framework
  • No images or video are captured, stored, or transmitted
  • Camera is active only while the Game is in the foreground
💾

Locally Stored Data

The Game stores the following data locally on your device to maintain your progress:

  • Game progress (level, XP, achievements, discoveries)
  • Currency balance and detector upgrades
  • Battery and free dig status
  • Privacy consent and analytics preferences

This data is protected with HMAC-SHA256 integrity checks to prevent tampering. It is never uploaded to any external service.

🛒

In-App Purchases

The Game offers optional in-app purchases through Google Play's billing system. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play — we do not access, store, or process any payment information (credit card numbers, billing addresses, etc.).

Transaction IDs from completed purchases are stored locally on your device solely for the purpose of purchase deduplication and restoration.

📊

Analytics (Optional)

On first launch, you are asked whether you consent to anonymous analytics. If you decline, no analytics data is collected.

If enabled, we may use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage events (e.g., session starts, tutorial completion). This data contains no personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve the Game. You can change your analytics preference at any time in the Game settings.

👶

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the Game, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

🔄

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
